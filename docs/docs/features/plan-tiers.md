# Plan Tiers

Choose the WhisperHedge plan that fits your needs.

## Available Plans

### Free Tier
**$0/month**

Perfect for getting started:
- Up to $10,000 TVL
- Up to 3 positions
- 15-minute updates
- Email notifications
- Basic support

### Pro Tier
**$29/month**

For active LPs:
- Up to $100,000 TVL
- Up to 10 positions
- 5-minute updates
- Email + Pushover notifications
- Priority support

### Premium Tier
**$99/month**

For professionals:
- Unlimited TVL
- Unlimited positions
- 1-minute updates
- All notification channels
- Premium support
- Advanced analytics (coming soon)

## Upgrading

### How to Upgrade

1. Go to [Manage Plan](https://whisperhedge.com/manage-plan)
2. Select your desired tier
3. Complete payment via Stripe
4. Instant activation

### Payment

- Secure payment via Stripe
- Monthly billing
- Cancel anytime
- No long-term contracts

### What Happens After Upgrade

- Immediate access to new limits
- Faster update frequency
- Additional features enabled
- No data migration needed

## Downgrading

### How to Downgrade

1. Go to Manage Plan
2. Select lower tier
3. Confirm downgrade
4. Takes effect next billing cycle

### If You Exceed New Limits

- Positions continue tracking
- Updates pause until within limits
- Remove positions to resume
- No data loss

## FAQs

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes, upgrade instantly or downgrade at next billing cycle.

**Q: What if I exceed my TVL limit?**
A: Position updates pause. Upgrade or remove positions to resume.

**Q: Is there a discount for annual billing?**
A: Not yet, but coming soon!

**Q: Can I try Pro/Premium before paying?**
A: Contact support for a trial period.
