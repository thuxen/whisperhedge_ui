# Data Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## What We Collect

### Required Data
- Email address (for authentication)
- API keys (encrypted, for position tracking)
- Position data (from your connected accounts)

### Optional Data
- Notification preferences
- Plan tier information
- Usage analytics

## What We Don't Collect

- ❌ Passwords (we use passwordless authentication)
- ❌ Private keys
- ❌ Trading history beyond tracked positions
- ❌ Personal information (name, address, etc.)

## How We Use Your Data

- Monitor your LP positions
- Calculate impermanent loss
- Send notifications
- Improve our service

## Data Security

- Encrypted at rest and in transit
- Regular security audits
- Minimal data retention
- Secure deletion when you close your account

## Your Rights

- View your data
- Export your data
- Delete your account
- Opt out of analytics

For more details, see our [Privacy Policy](https://whisperhedge.com/privacy).
